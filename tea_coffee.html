Introduction

The client is the owner of a coffee and tea garden who produces coffee and black tea in Hualien and his products are popular because of the reputable tea shop.
For the first beverage, a combination of black tea and coffee, I designed five proposals. The client chose the visual design with clear images of black tea and coffee because the customer can easily understand what the ingredients are. I also created a series of goods with gold foil printing. The second product is honey black tea, which is one of the signatures products in the tea shop. The packaging design is a revised version of the original proposal for the first beverage. As for the gift box of the popular souvenir, my concept was to create a festive atmosphere.
