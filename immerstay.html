Introduction

TA of the iMMERSTAY is for foreigners who came to Taiwan and looked for long term accommodation. There are many kinds of rooms, and users can find rooms provided by host families of residents.

I was in charge of the visual of these pages and discussed with a designer for the interface of index and be a host pages.

Conception

Due to this kind of platforms are not exist, the similar one is Airbnb, however, Airbnb is the worldwide accommodation platform, not for the specific area. That is why we did immerstay only for users in Taiwan. Furthermore, compare with Airbnb, the most special function is “tenant's wishing well” the applicants can post their requirements (Non-smoker, No kids, No pet, Not student, Vegan) and the space they want, and then the hosts and guests will be paired.

The impact

At first, immerstay was an unknown platform in Taiwan, nevertheless, the usage rate is higher year by year. During the depression, we took some user interviews of NCKU students, then delete these useless functions to highlight the important functions. I think immerstay will be the next Taiwanese Airbnb in the future.
