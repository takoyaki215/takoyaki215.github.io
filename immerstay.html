Introduction

TA of the iMMERSTAY is for foreigners who come to Taiwan and are looking for long term accommodation. There are many kinds of accommodation, and users can find rooms provided by host families of residents.

What I did...

I was in charge of the visuals of these pages and discussed with a designer the interface of index and host pages.

User Concerns

Foreign Tenants
  • Overcome language and rental period duration barriers.
Taiwanese Hosts
  • I have a property for rent.
  • I like making friends from different cultures.

Tool

Icon

Design Conceptualization

In Taiwan no such platforms exist. However, Airbnb is similar except it is a worldwide accommodation platform, not focusing on a specific area. That is why we did iMMERSTAY only for users in Taiwan. Furthermore, when compared with Airbnb, there is a special function, “tenant's wishing well” that allows applicants to post their requirements (Non-smoker, No kids, No pet, Not student, Vegan) and the space they want, and then the hosts and guests will be paired.

Impact

Initially, iMMERSTAY was an unknown platform in Taiwan, nevertheless, the usage rate became higher year by year. iMMERSTAY will become a Taiwanese Airbnb in the future.
