Immerstay

immerstay sharing house website - UX and UI / 2016

TEAM WORK

The target of iMMERSTAY is for the foreigners who came to Taiwan to look for a long stay. There are many kinds of rooms, and native people can be the host family to utilize the unnecessary rooms. Furthermore, in the tenant's wishing well, if the applicants would like to post their requirements and space, both the host and guest will be paired.
I worked with another designer to discuss the interface and the flow.
