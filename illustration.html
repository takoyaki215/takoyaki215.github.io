Pattern of enamel cup - Taiwanese dog

2016 / INDEPENDENT WORK

The enamel cup I designed will be exported to Northern Europe. The business owner would like to use “dog” as the theme of the product. Based on his request, I drew some Taiwan dogs and pineapples and portrayed dogs as playing in a pineapple farm. The illustration gives off happiness and tropical vibe.

Environmental protection poster - 3R

2010 / INDEPENDENT WORK

3R - Reduce Reuse Recycle
The main idea of the poster is that water resource can be used sustainably. People can directly use the natural water resource through the tap because the wasted water already passes through the treatment and purification equipment. After that, the water flows into the river and it can be reused because of the good water recycling system.

Aboriginal Poster - Taiwanese aborigine and music

2010 / INDEPENDENT WORK

This poster was created when I studied at the university. My inspiration came from Taiwanese aboriginals. They enjoy hunting, dancing, and singing; therefore, I used sheet music to create the illustration of the happy aboriginals dancing around the fire and roasting a wild boar to celebrate their traditional festival.
