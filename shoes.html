Footwear Box/Catalog

Taiwanese footwear factory - graphic and packing design

2011, 2014 / INDEPENDENT WORK

I designed various versions of shoe boxes based on different age groups. The shoe catalogs were offered to the department stores for business purpose.
For the 2011 catalogs, I cooperated with a photographer to create a sweet and dreamy style for female teenagers.
For the 2014 catalogs, since the target clients were office ladies, I used roses to create an elegant style. As for the male shoes, I created a yuppie style with a minimalist layout.
