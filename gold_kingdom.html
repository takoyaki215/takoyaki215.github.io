Gold Kingdom

Gold Kingdom Website - Front-end (Vue and CSS)

2019 / TEAMWORK

In this project, I worked as a front-end developer, so that CSS of an index, customize and back-end is my duty by using a new javascript framework- Vue.
For appreciate the god, the most meaningful are to customize the golden plate with the name of mine. Moreover, modern people ask for god to fillful their wishes by making golden plates that kind of worship for acknowledgment. The gold of the website is to sale golden plates on these special religion days, and the feature for users to design their golden plates by themselves. Not only to sell golden plates but also spread the concept of traditional Taiwanese Buddha culture and to sell back the gold.
