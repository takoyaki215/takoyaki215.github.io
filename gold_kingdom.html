Gold Kingdom Sacrificial Website

Conception

The gold of the website is to sale golden plates on these special religion days, and the feature for users to design their golden plates by themselves. Not only to sell golden plates but also spread the concept of traditional Taiwanese Buddha culture and to sell back the gold.

Problem & Solution

In Taiwan, followers of Taoism and Buddhism sometimes will offer donations to temples that act as sacrifices to beseech the deities of a favor. To do so, they may purchase a golden name plate to add physically somewhere to the architecture of the temple. This website template offers temple’s the ability to elicit orders for such name plates online.

Design And Impact

The function of customizing the gold plate solves time-saving and communicated problems of both clients and gold plate holders. Gold kingdom website offers a modern way for everyone to buy gold fast and conveniently. Furthermore, not only the loyalty purchasers for traditional events but also young customers purchase gold plates for gifts.
