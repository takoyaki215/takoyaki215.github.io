Administrator Webpage & App UI

Problem & Solution

Temples are ubiquitous in Taiwan, perhaps only outnumbered by convenience stores. However, little to zero technological aid has been prepared to assist in their function and administration. Therefore, this project’s aim was to create a website to facilitate creation of to-do lists, tables, forms, and data in regard to temple followers, track the financial affairs of temples, and to create a calendar to coordinate activities with others temples. Each feature phase of this website is serialized, starting with its design and development by using a bootstrap.

user insight

As an accountant, the most important thing is that to show receipt and disbursement statements clearly.

As an owner, introducing my temple's features through the app attracting more young generation believers. Moreover, the aesthetics admin panel can help me to build new business relationships
