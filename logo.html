Motorcycle Modification Shop

The owner of the motorcycle-modifying shop requested to use velociraptors for the logo, so I illustrated a velociraptor holding a wrench. For the business cards, I used the tire track as the pattern of the background.

Nutrition Startup

A logo, an icon of the app and a mascot of a nutrition startup called “Cofit.” The first picture on the left side is an American healthy eating plate, and I referred to its colors and composition to create the new icon.

Leather Company

The company requested to use a crocodile for the logo, so I thought of the ancient Egyptian deity, Sobek, who has the image of crocodiles and represents power and fertility. I used his form, a human with a crocodile head, as the logo, and named the brand after Sobek.
