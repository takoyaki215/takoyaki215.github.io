Introduction

This APP enables the students of National Cheng Kung University (NCKU) to use the discount system in the affiliated stores.
The merchants need to use it via scanning the QR code of the students. This project of blockchain discount system can be redeemed by the reward points, which attracts the students and the merchants to keep using this APP for the transaction.

As a designer on this finch project( merchants ), I provide UI/UX aid to other teams and also create and maintain standards for the design system library and translate product features for app’s context.

Conception

At first, NCKU proved points to management parts of students. Accordingly, students pay via the app with points or cash as a transaction, and every time can accumulate some points in which the exchange rate is decided by the school. Not only those hose points can also be translated by users of merchants but also they log in admin via personal computer to manage the data. The major roles of the index are New Order, Quick Pay, Order Mgmt and Trans Setting.

▾ Order
  • ▾ New Order- I design the tutorial for first-time users to lead them Product” of the bottom tab, then they have to create a new catalog (fruit) and tap into a list to add new items (apple, banana or orange…etc). Merchant user also has a choice, to set the difference discounts for students in Trans Setting. After that, they can use the function of "New Order" to screen the QR code of the student’s app.
  • Quick Pay - Only screen QR code then finish a payment, the disadvantage is that it can not offer discounts for students.
  • Order Mgmt - They can search and export the records before, and the refund service also in there.
  • Trans Setting - Dependent on merchants, to setting discounts as a reward for students, or to keep the original price.
