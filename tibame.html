Tibame

Tibame online learning website - UI and Graphic Design / 2014-2015

UI DESIGN / TEAM WORK

Tibame is a website system, which is for Taiwanese online learning for coding, and was built by the Institute for Information Industry (III) and campuscruiser (CC).
When I took over the project, I designed the new index and add incorporated interactions into it; furthermore, I improved not only front-end but the back-end of the flow and interface. Including the design and programming, I revised the CSS and HTML with international colleagues (from New York and Shanghai), engineers and the product manager. To achieve the needs of users through the meetings, I created the prototype and mockup for the web.
Because campuscruiser (CC) is the online educational system company, I designed the icon from an owl, which forms the abbreviation.
