Introduction

The air conditioner and dehumidifier are only two smart appliances in New Wide Tech at that time. Both of them are equipped with sensors to connect to the cloud. The most special function is the notification for the users to know the condition of the machine and to bill calculation. These notifications feature make it convenient for users who are quite far from the appliances.Furthermore, bill calculation function could calculate the electricity bill automatically.

I worked on this project as an UI Designer for smart home team of new wide tech. I was responsible for the research, UI/UX design, visual design.

Conception

Knowing the importance of aesthetics and practicability make me to ask myself how the app fits into the lives of the users. However, the UI of air conditioner app is created by engineersat on the first version, it is why I have to modify the user interface. Therefore, the ideal user experiences and focused on how users think and behave for my goals. As a result, I set both goals for the short-term and long-term.

▾ Before- AC's home/electricity bill
▾ After- AC's home

For the short-term goal, to improve the aesthetics is the most important issue in the air conditioner app. Therefore, all of the positions and functions are the same as before, I only modified the color and icons of UI, then I add tutorial during connection for more user-friendly.

Practicability is the major role in an app, that way I would like to be concordant with three appliances- air condition, dehumidifier, and air purifier, this idea is also my long-term goal. Plus, I would like to add “admin” to manage the other family users, then they can switch to any of the appliances.
“appliance management” - through this function, users can switch to any of the appliances, and the admin interface page shows temperature, humidity, and air quality as well.
“user management” - for an only admin user, to decide to who can operate these appliances.
