Introduction

I designed the multiple applications of a summer project on a mobile UX design course at SVA NY. The theme was "eat", and there were two kinds of UI: smartphone and Apple TV. For the application, I created the platform app for people from New York, who would like to have BBQs on the rooftop.

Customer Insights

  • There is no rooftop on my apartment, but I want to join BBQ party in summer!
Resident with rooftop P.

Conception

Combing through my research and discovery, there are a lot of skyscrapers in New York, it is popular to throw a barbecue party with friends on the rooftop to enjoy during the limited summer time. As a result, if you reside in an apartment with a rooftop, you can invite guests or friends via my Rooftop BBQ app.

▾ “activity”( Main page ) - It displays every BBQ activities in all of New York (The Bronx, Brooklyn, Manhattan, Queens, and Staten Island ), there are two options- be a host or be an Invitee. When users tap into one of the activities, they can cheat with inviter and know the exact location. After they agree to join the activity, they have to sign up and pay for that. Finally, the RBQ app shows some transportation information.
▾ “profile” - this page divide into three parts- join, host, and save
  • join - it shows how many activities that users joined and their transportation information.
  • host - it is the place that a host might modify the information and edit it.
  • save - those activities are which users already press the heart button and interest in.
