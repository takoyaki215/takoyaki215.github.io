eID APP UI (Proposal)

Problem

There are two situations of the app:
1. To show my information to the government staff.
2. Taiwanese people who lost or stolen a new e-identity card need to show many documents to the government to apply for a new ID card.

user insight

I am worried about my personal information data breach if I lost my mobile phone.

I don't need to bring a pile of documents to the government office, only take a smartphone!

What I Did...

The process is designed regarding laws and regulations, and the larger contribution is the part of the visual interface. My main work in this project is UI for iOS and Android systems. I think the biggest challenge in this project is patience. When designing the process, you must repeatedly confirm and modify with the supervisor to complete the wireframe. To make it easy for users to get started, I followed a manual, carefully read page by page, and visualize the lengthy text through the image.
