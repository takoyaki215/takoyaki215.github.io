Year

Introduction

It was a proposal for the cryptocurrency project in the company. We used the APP of the Fintech as the foundation and modified it, and I was in charge of the UI. I used orange as the key color collocated with deep background-colors, which is popular now. According to the Material Design guidelines, I used different grey colors to create visual hierarchy. The orange, black and grey are just the colors of tigers, so I used tigers as the mascots. I put the mascot in different screens to make them delightful and not dull.

Conception

The situation is that both teachers and students have an iPad.
Because students are physically and mentally challenged children, the actions have to divide into several detailed steps to finish. For example, hand washing needs to be divided into several steps from applying soap for drying hands. I used tropical elements and animals to attract children’s interest and make special education no longer dreary and establish a unique style. The gamification design encourages children to complete the tasks.
