Tin Coin Proposal

tin coin proposal - UI / 2019

2019 / INDEPENDENT WORK

公司內部加密貨幣專案的提案，以Finteck的APP做基底修改，我負責設計UI。以橘黃色為主色，搭配現在的趨勢-深色底，參考material design guideline 用不同的灰色堆疊出層次感，剛好符合老虎的配色，所以使用老虎為吉祥物，在不同的畫面中添加，使畫面活潑有趣不呆板。

It was a proposal for the cryptocurrency project in the company. We used the APP of the Fintech as the foundation and modified it, and I was in charge of the UI. I used orange as the key color collocated with deep background-colors, which is popular now. According to the Material Design guidelines, I used different grey colors to create visual hierarchy. The orange, black and grey are just the colors of tigers, so I used tigers as the mascots. I put the mascot in different screens to make them delightful and not dull.
