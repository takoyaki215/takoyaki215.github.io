HRM system

Problem

Instead of a traditional punch card machine and complicated PC system, this HRM system app wants to simplify office processes for employee users. Using this app saves not only the punch card papers but also the human resource cost.
In 2016, the population of mobile users was more than PCs. The latest figures show an increasing number of smartphone users year after year. In 2021, the number of global smartphone users is estimated at 6.4 billion, marking a 5.3% annual increase.(https://www.oberlo.com/statistics/how-many-people-have-smartphones)
Therefore, to salve the ecology problem and efficiency is following the trend of this modern generation.

user insight

As a remote work employee, I would like to diminish my time to deal with complicated processes.

I don't want to stand in a line to punch a card, and it might affect my perfect attendance, then the bonus is gone...

Inspiration

Because most of the employee users are Taiwanese, and Japanese mangas affected these generations very much, that is the reason why I got inspiration from the mangas to design the illustrations that to catch users' attention.

What I Did...

Convergent thinking makes me pick up the essential functions to design the app. The most frequent employee action is punch cards, and the second one is taking leave; therefore, I put these two functions as the primary point in the app. Simple flow and large buttons make users easy to understand then use the app without thinking so much.
