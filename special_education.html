Introduction

The users are the teachers and the students of the schools in America. I was responsible for the UI, UX, and visual design, and collaborated with IOS engineers. I gradually understood customers’ needs through continuous communication. The customers still use pen and paper to communicate with their students now. Such communication is like making to-do lists for these students and supervising them to finish it, and that is where my inspiration comes from.
Because some of the users are physically and mentally challenged children, the font and line spacing need to be larger. Those children can be guided step by step to complete basic movements through the breakdown of each movement. For example, hand washing needs to be divided into several steps from applying soap for drying hands, and there are not only videos, pictures or sounds cues of each step, but also congratulation pages after they complete. The gamification design encourages children to complete the tasks. The dashboard for teachers is composed of timetable, history, and template which is easy to administer. I also used tropical elements and animals to attract children’s interest and make special education no longer dreary and establish a unique style.

Users

Teacher S.

She has a bachelor’s degree in special education and works in public schools, with students ranging from preschool through high school. S. works highly rewarding, but also emotionally demanding and physically draining. After a few years, she would like to get more licenses in an education-related field.

Student A.

A. will show improved pre-handwriting skills by being able to make a circle, cross, and square with visual cues for starting point or corners in 4 out of 5 trials as measured by work samples.

Student B.

B. will show improved attending skills to participate and complete at least one no more than 5 minutes long non-preferred activity before needing a break, with less than 3 attempts of escape during the activity, in 3 out of 5 trials as observed by classroom staffs of therapist.
